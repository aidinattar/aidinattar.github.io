## Bachelor’s Thesis

~~~
<div class="card thesis-card">
  <span class="card-meta">Bachelor's Thesis</span>
  <h3>Search of gravitational waves signal due to post merger phase of neutron star binary merger</h3>
  <p><strong>University of Padova</strong></p>
  <p>
    Bachelor’s thesis in Physics focused on the detection and reconstruction of post-merger
    gravitational-wave signals from binary neutron star systems. The work centers on the
    <em>Coherent WaveBurst</em> algorithm and on simulated injections in the LIGO-Virgo network,
    with attention to how well post-coalescence dynamics can be recovered under O4 sensitivity
    assumptions.
  </p>
  <p>
    The manuscript is written in Italian and is available through the University of Padova archive.
  </p>
  <p><a href="https://hdl.handle.net/20.500.12608/21200">Read thesis (Italian)</a></p>
</div>
~~~
