  <h1>Graduating with my Master's degree</h1>
  <p class="note-intro__meta"><em>December 6, 2024</em></p>
</section>
~~~

Today, I officially graduated with my Master's degree in Physics of Data from the University of Padova.
It was a demanding but deeply rewarding path, one that shaped the way I think about data, modeling,
and research more broadly.

What I value most, looking back, is not only the final milestone but the process itself: the difficult exams,
the long hours of study, the technical skills built over time, and the gradual realization that I wanted to keep
working on ambitious problems at the intersection of theory, computation, and learning.

This degree marked the end of an important chapter and, at the same time, the beginning of a new one.
I am grateful for the people, opportunities, and challenges that made the journey meaningful.
